---
name: rcp-project-doctor
description: Inspect Reality Composer Pro and RealityKit projects for structure, assets, build integration, and risk signals.
---

Run `rcp_project_doctor` at the project root. Look for `.xcodeproj`, `.xcworkspace`, `.realitycomposerpro`, `.rkassets`, Swift packages, RealityKitContent bundles, USD assets, oversized media, and generated outputs.

Report blockers before editing and suggest a safe validation command.
